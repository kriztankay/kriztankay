About me

My name is Krisztián Kökössy. I was born in Keszthely, Hungary and lived the most of my life there. After finishing High School in 2018, I moved in Germany the same year. I'm studying as Audio Engineer. I've been making music for several years, in the following Genres: Progressive House, Future House, Deep House. I have plenty of new music / projects on the way. My goal is to spread my music to more and more people to share my emotions and thoughts through my Art. I'm open to any artist who would like to work together or alongside with me. Follow me, to be up to date, and BE PREPARED!
